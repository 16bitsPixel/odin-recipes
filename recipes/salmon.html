Salmon

Description

The dish we are creating is a garlic lemon baked salmon. This is a very light and healthy dish, perfect for dieting. The lemon and garlic perfectly complement the buttery soft salmon.
The dish is very simple to make, requiring only a few ingredients. This salmon is perfect to pair with some green vegetables.
The key to this decision is to not dry out the salmon. As long as it's not overcooked, you'll have the perfect fish dish that everyone will love!

Ingredients

  • Salmon Fillets
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic and Dried Thyme
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic into small pieces
  2. Create small diagonal surface slices on the top of the salmon
  3. Stuff garlic into pockets we created from the slices
  4. Coat fish fillets with olive oil
  5. Sprinkle salt, pepper, and thyme all over the salmon
  6. Slice the lemon into thin slices
  7. Place the lemon slices on top
  8. Put salmon on a baking dish, cover with foil and bake until cooked through
